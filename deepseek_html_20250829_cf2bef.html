Featured Recipe

Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Servings: 2
Calories: 320

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Toast the whole grain bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado and mash with a fork.
  4. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer. Crack eggs into small cups and carefully slide into the water.
  5. Poach eggs for 3-4 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  6. Spread the avocado mixture on toast, top with poached eggs, and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.
